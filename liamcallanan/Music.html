8-Bit Flashback

					
This was one of my first songs made using a plugin called the "Magical 8-Bit Plug". I thought it would be interesting to work with basic video game sounds and tweak them around. I also experimented a little bit with varying tempo.


				


				


					

					
Fade_2

					
This one is really the result of experimentation with volume fading. I wanted to make the transitions sound natural and the progression gradual, which is sometimes difficult with 8-bit sounds.


				


				


					

					
24:00:00

					
I decided to stick with only the basic sounds for this one: no special sound modifiers or volume transitions. I wanted to focus more on melody for this one. It's a bit grainy, but I like it anyways.


				
				


				


					

					
Matrix_

					
I made this one as a project for my Electronic Music class in my Junior year of high school. It went along with a clip from the Matrix, which is where I got the name. As usual, I used the 8-bit plug for most sounds.


				
				


				


					

					
Open Road 8-Bit

					
After working with the 8-bit plug for a while, I realized that the texture of those kinds of sounds were often very harsh. With this song, I tried to soften those high frequencies and make the treble more smooth.
